VibeCheck Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-16
Version: 1.0

This Privacy Policy is based on the current source code and applies to the VibeCheck Android app.

1. Data We Process
- Ledger data: transaction type, amount, timestamp, notes, mood score.
- App settings: language preference, onboarding completion state.

2. Data Sources
- All data is produced by your explicit actions in the app.

3. Purposes of Use
- Display bill list, dashboard, and analytics.
- Persist your language and onboarding state for a consistent experience.

4. Storage Location & Method
- Ledger data is stored in the app private file: accounting_records.txt.
- Settings data is stored in the app private SharedPreferences: vibe_prefs.
- Your data stays on your device by default and is not uploaded to any server.

5. Network & Third-Party Sharing
- No account system, cloud sync, or server upload is present in this version.
- No advertising, analytics, or crash reporting SDKs are present.
- We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

6. Permissions & Device Access
- The AndroidManifest does not declare sensitive permissions (contacts, location, camera, microphone, etc.).
- The app operates within local files and local settings only.

7. Backup & Migration
- With android:allowBackup set to false, system automatic backup is disabled.
- Success of device migration via system/vendor tools depends on their implementation and policies.

8. Your Control
- Use “Reset Data” in Settings to delete all ledger records.
- Uninstalling the app typically removes its private directory data.

9. Updates to This Policy
- If future versions introduce network, accounts, sync, analytics, or ads, this policy will be updated accordingly.

10. Contact
- Questions? Please open an issue at https://github.com/syweb-dev/VIBECHECK-Android/issues
